Steamed Mussels

Description

Indulge in a delightful dish of steamed mussels, bathed in a fragrant broth of garlic, onions, cherry tomatoes, and a splash of white wine. This savory ensemble, seasoned to perfection and garnished with fresh parsley, offers a taste of the sea in every succulent, shellfish-infused bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds fresh mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (otpional, for some heat)
  • Crusty Bread, for serving

Steps

  1. Rinse the mussels under cold water, scrubbing off any debris. Remove the beards (the stringy bits) from the mussels.
  2. Chop the garlic, onion, and parsley. Halve the cherry tomatoes.
  3. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Saut√© until softened and fragrant.
  4. Add the halved cherry tomatoes to the pot. Pour in the white wine and chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Season the broth with salt, pepper, and optional crushed red pepper flakes for some heat. Add the cleaned mussels to the pot.
  6. Cover the pot with a lid and steam the mussels for about 5-7 minutes or until they open. Discard any mussels that do not open.
  7. Once the mussels are cooked, sprinkle chopped parsley over them. Serve the steamed mussels in bowls, with the broth, and accompanied by crusty bread for soaking up the delicious broth.
