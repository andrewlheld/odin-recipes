Banana Bread

Description

Savor the perfect blend of ripe bananas, sweetness, and a touch of cinnamon with our easy-to-make homemade banana bread. Ideal for breakfast or a sweet indulgence, this moist and tender loaf is a guaranteed family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 4 Ripe bananas
  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Granulated sugar
  • 6 table spoons Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of Cinnamon(optional)
  • 1 cup of Chopped nuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork.
  3. Melt the butter in a microwave-safe bowl or on the stovetop. Add the melted butter to the mashed bananas and mix well.
  4. Stir the baking soda into the mashed bananas.
  5. Add a pinch of salt, beaten egg, and vanilla extract to the banana mixture. Mix until well combined.
  6. Gradually add the flour to the wet ingredients, stirring until just incorporated. Do not overmix.
  7. If desired, fold in cinnamon and chopped nuts or chocolate chips.
  8. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan, spreading it evenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Once cooled, slice and enjoy your delicious homemade banana bread!
